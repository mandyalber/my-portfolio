About Me

I began my journey into coding when I was looking to find a job that wouldn't leave me watching the clock all day. While I enjoyed my roles as an accounting professional and a data specialist, I was searching for something that would allow me more flexibility, autonomy and the opportunity to continually learn new things.

After dabbling online and then taking a few college classes, I was hooked! As a Thinkful student, I am reaping the benefits of collaborating with a mentor and other students to propel me to my next career.

I have a Web Programming Certificate that demonstrates my experience with HTML, CSS and JavaScript, as well as exposure to Java, C++, PHP, SQL and Joomla! CMS (Content Management System). I am continually learning new things and look forward to creating quality projects.

Services I would like to provide:

  4. Third-Party Integration & Customization
